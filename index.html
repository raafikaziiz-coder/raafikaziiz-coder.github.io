SR Hunter V4

SR Hunter V4 is a layered Pine Script indicator designed for TradingView. It helps validate trading setups through structured market analysis instead of blind signals.

SR Hunter V4 adalah indikator Pine Script berlapis untuk TradingView. Tools ini membantu memvalidasi setup trading melalui analisis market yang terstruktur, bukan sekadar sinyal buy atau sell.
